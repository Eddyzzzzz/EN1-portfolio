Heading

When the color sensor senses the specified color, the first motor will move to the specified position, and the second motor will drive a claw to grasp the ball; When the first motor rotates 180 degrees, the second motor will rotate in the opposite direction, release the claw to put down the ball, and finally my robotic arm will move to a far position so that the next person's project can run smoothly.  

Keeping the speed at 10% throughout the whole process makes my project more stable and has a higher success rate.
